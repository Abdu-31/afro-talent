About Our Agency

Bridging Ethiopia and the Middle East with Integrity

AfroTalent is a fully licensed overseas employment agency headquartered in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We specialize in the ethical recruitment, training, and deployment of domestic workers to households and agencies across Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman.

Every candidate we place undergoes rigorous background verification, medical screening, language training, and cultural orientation — ensuring a smooth, successful placement for both worker and employer.

  • Verified Candidates — Full identity, criminal, and reference checks
  • Professional Training — Housekeeping, childcare, cooking, elder care
  • Post-Placement Support — 24/7 assistance for workers and employers
  • Legal Compliance — Full adherence to Ethiopian & GCC labor laws

Worker Protection

Guaranteed Rights & Protections

We ensure every worker is fully informed of their rights and protected throughout their employment contract.

✓ Legal Employment Contract

Bilingual contract (English/Arabic) clearly stating salary, working hours, rest days, and benefits — registered with authorities.

✓ Timely Salary Payment

Guaranteed monthly salary payment as per contract, with full transparency on any deductions. No hidden fees.

✓ Proper Accommodation

Private, clean, and safe living space with adequate food and basic amenities as specified in the employment contract.

✓ Medical Care

Full medical insurance coverage and access to healthcare services throughout the contract period.

✓ Rest Days & Leave

Minimum one rest day per week and 30 days paid annual leave as per GCC labor laws and contract terms.

✓ 24/7 Emergency Support

Access to our multilingual helpline and local welfare officers for any issues — personal, professional, or legal.

✓ Freedom of Communication

Right to keep passport copies (employer holds original only with worker's consent), use phone, and communicate with family.

✓ Repatriation Guarantee

Return flight ticket to Ethiopia at the end of contract or in case of contract termination by employer.
